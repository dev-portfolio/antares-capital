Fully Renovated | Custom-Designed | Villa

This exceptional Signature villa has been fully renovated with superb quality detailing to create an awe-inspiring beachfront luxury property. The villa comes with a vast open plan living area that has Italian book match marble flooring as well as impressive views over the blue waters and the iconic Atlantis hotels. The Goettling kitchen is equipped with Siemens appliances and a Quooker tap. Other notable brands in the villa include Bagno, Lutron, Gracili, Cleaf, Blum, Euroart and Hafele.

The villa also has a Daikin climate control system, an internal lift to move between floors, a private gym, sauna and steam room. The villa's exterior elements are particularly outstanding and include a BBQ counter, automated awnings, outdoor fridge and fireplace and a heated swimming pool with 300 fibre optic lights and a jacuzzi. All of these are steps away from the private beach and overlook the superb views across the sea to the Atlantis resorts.

Frond K is one of the exclusive gated communities on Palm Jumeirah, giving residents untold privacy and security. With the villa's excellent amenities and private beach access, residents will not want for much, but they will find themselves only moments from the dazzling array of venues and outlets on Palm Jumeirah including its five-star hotels, fine dining restaurants and excellent shops.
